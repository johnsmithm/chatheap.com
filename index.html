Frequently Asked Questions

How does ChatHeap work?

ChatHeap monitors your LinkedIn posts for specific keywords in comments. When someone comments with the keyword, it automatically sends them your lead magnet via DM and can initiate a connection request if you're not connected.

Is it safe to use with LinkedIn?

Yes, ChatHeap uses secure cookie-based authentication and respects LinkedIn's daily limits. We use human-like delays and randomization to ensure natural behavior.

What kind of lead magnets can I use?

Any downloadable content works great - PDFs, templates, checklists, whitepapers, case studies, or video training links. The key is providing value that resonates with your target audience.

Can I customize the DM messages?

Yes! You can create multiple message variants and ChatHeap will randomly select them to maintain a natural feel. You can also set up follow-up sequences.

What happens after the trial?

After your 7-day trial, you can choose any of our plans. Your first month is only $9! If you decide not to continue, your account will be automatically deactivated - no surprise charges.
